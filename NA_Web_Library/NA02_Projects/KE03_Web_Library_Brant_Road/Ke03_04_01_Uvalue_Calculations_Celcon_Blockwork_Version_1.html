This report details a breakdown of the layers making up the specified element, it will detail each of the respective layers thermal efficiency properties. To ensure compliance with Approve Document Part-L, refer to the charts below, which outline the U-value calculations for the entire element assembly.

  • U-values are calculated in accordance with BS EN ISO 6946 standards.
  • R-values have been sourced from publicly available charts and product specification sheets.
  • Minor rounding discrepancies may occur in calculations, with rounding applied in the final figures.
  • All calculations are based on the assumption that materials are in a dry condition.
